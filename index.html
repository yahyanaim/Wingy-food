Step 1:Download and Install the App
Start by downloading the app from your device's app store. Once downloaded, follow the installation instructions. Launch the app and sign up or log in to your account.

Step 2: Set Up Your Profile
Complete your profile by providing essential details such as your delivery address, contact number, and any specific delivery instructions. This ensures a seamless ordering process and accurate delivery.

Step 3: Browse Restaurants and Menus
Explore the variety of restaurants available on the app. Use the search and filter options to narrow down your choices based on cuisine, price range, or customer ratings. Browse through menus to find your desired dishes.

Step 4: Select Your Items
Once you've chosen a restaurant, browse their menu and add items to your cart. Specify any preferences or modifications to your order, such as special instructions for the chef or dietary requirements.

Step 5: Review Your Order
Before proceeding to checkout, carefully review your order to ensure accuracy. Verify the items in your cart, quantities, and any additional charges. Make adjustments if necessary.

Step 6: Place Your Order
Click on the checkout button to place your order. Choose your preferred payment method, whether it's credit/debit card, digital wallets, or cash on delivery. Confirm your order, and you'll receive a confirmation notification.

Step 7: Track Your Delivery
Once your order is confirmed, you can track its status in real-time. Receive updates on the preparation, dispatch, and estimated delivery time. This feature keeps you informed and reduces uncertainty.

Step 8: Receive Your Order
As your order approaches its destination, you'll receive a notification. Meet your delivery driver at the specified location, and enjoy your freshly delivered meal. Don't forget to rate your experience and provide feedback on the app.

By following these steps, you'll be able to navigate our app effortlessly, ensuring a delightful experience from the moment you browse the menu to the satisfaction of enjoying your favorite dishes at home.

For Restaurants:

Step 1: Reach Out to Us
Contact our business partnership team through our website or designated business portal. Share basic information about your restaurant, such as its location, menu specialties, and any unique offerings.

Step 2: Verification Process
Upon receiving your inquiry, our team will initiate a verification process to ensure your restaurant meets our standards. This may include checking licenses, food safety practices, and reviewing your menu.

Step 1: Integration and Onboarding
Once approved, our technical team will guide you through the integration process. This involves linking your menu to our platform, setting up your restaurant profile, and familiarizing you with our order management system.

Step 3: Menu Customization
Work with our team to optimize your menu for our platform. Highlight popular items, set prices, and update availability to ensure customers receive accurate and enticing information.

Step 4: Training
Our team will provide comprehensive training on using our partner portal. This includes order processing, tracking, and managing customer feedback. We'll ensure you're well-equipped to handle orders seamlessly.

Step 5: Go Live
Once everything is set up, your restaurant will go live on our platform. Customers will be able to discover, browse, and order from your menu. Prepare your team for increased online orders and deliveries.

For Delivery Drivers:

Step 1: Apply Online
Visit our website or app and navigate to the "Become a Delivery Driver" section. Fill out the application form with your personal details, vehicle information, and relevant documents.

Step 2: Document Verification
Our team will review your application and verify your documents, including your driver's license, vehicle registration, and insurance. This process ensures compliance with safety and regulatory standards.

Step 3: Orientation
Attend an orientation session to familiarize yourself with our delivery procedures, safety protocols, and customer service expectations. This step is crucial to providing a positive and consistent experience for our customers.

Step 4: Activate Your Account
Once your application is approved and you've completed the orientation, your delivery driver account will be activated. You'll gain access to our delivery platform and start receiving delivery requests.

Step 5: Delivery Execution
Accept delivery requests through the app, pick up orders from partner restaurants, and deliver them to customers. Use the app's navigation features to optimize your route and ensure timely deliveries.

Step 6: Feedback and Ratings
Maintain a high level of service to receive positive feedback and ratings from customers. Consistent performance may lead to additional incentives and opportunities.

By following these steps, both restaurants and delivery drivers can seamlessly integrate with our platform, providing customers with a reliable and efficient service. We look forward to building a successful partnership with you!
